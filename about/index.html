About me

Musician-turned-coder

I trained as a musician and musicologist and am now changing career path and pursuing my interest in coding. In January 2021, I began a full stack developer course with Northcoders.

I'm a fairly longstanding Linux user (Arch Linux, by the way) and suppose that was my 'gateway' to coding. I'm a big supporter of free and open source software and believe that software developers can play a major role in improving the world we live in.

I like making things and bringing elements together into something which is practical and meaningful. I also like making things happen; I founded a professional opera company and have a lot of project management experience.

Although I am very early on in my coding journey, I feel most drawn towards full stack web development and DevOps, and am hoping to find a role where I can continue to develop and challenge myself.

I fuel my coding with plenty of freshly brewed coffee from my Bialetti Moka Express (there is no better way to make coffee!), and – when possible – like to do long distance cycling.
