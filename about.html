About me

Musician-turned-coder

I trained as a musician and musicologist. I am now changing career path and pursuing my interest in logic and coding. In January 2021, I begin a full-stack developer course with Northcoders.

If you're interested, here's a little overview of some of my musical activities.

I'm a fairly longstanding Linux user and suppose that was my 'gateway drug' to coding. I'm a big believer in free and open source software. You'll find me across the web, usually as galambborong, but occasionally by my actual name. Other than LinkedIn, you'll not find me on any mainstream social media. I red-pilled a while back and am quite happy on the decentralised, open source Mastodon platform!
