A little bit about me

Musician-turned-coder

I have dipped my feet into coding once or twice over the years, but it was in April 2020 when Pluralsight was free for the month that I really discovered my interest all things code!

I trained as a pianist, composer and viola player at a music school before studying music at university. If you're interested in my musical activities, you can read here.

I've been feeling quite 'stuck' between trying to earn my keep and doing what I love. The discovery of coding seems to have struck a chord (no pun intended!) and I am excited to be moving into the fast-paced and exciting world of tech!
